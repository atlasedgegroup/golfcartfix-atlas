About CartFix

Golf cart repair guides built around the diagnostic sequence a technician actually uses — not a forum post from 2009.

What CartFix Is

CartFix is a diagnostic guide site for golf cart owners who want to understand what's wrong before spending money at a shop — or who want to fix it themselves and need clear procedures rather than vague forum advice.

Every guide on this site follows the same structure: fast answer first (the most likely fix, stated clearly), then a full diagnostic tree if the first check doesn't resolve it, then a parts cost table so you know what you're getting into. Affiliate links to Amazon are included where relevant — we earn a small commission if you buy through those links, at no extra cost to you.

What We Cover

We focus on the three brands that make up most of the US golf cart market: E-Z-GO (TXT and RXV), Club Car (DS and Precedent), and Yamaha (G-Series, Drive, and Drive2). Both gas and electric models. Both 36V and 48V systems.
